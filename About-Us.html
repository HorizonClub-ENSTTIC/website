What is Horizon club?

Horizon Club is a student organization at the Ecole Nationale Supérieure des Télécommunications et des Technologies de l'Information et de la Communication (ENSTTIC) in Oran, Algeria, established in November 2009.

What we do in Horizon club?

Horizon Club organizes annual events, with the most significant ones being Ideator and Alumni Day. Additionally, the club conducts training sessions in various fields, primarily focusing on Information and Communication Technologies (ICT). also covering workshops and entertainments.Moreover, the club actively engages in project work, reflecting a commitment to practical application and innovation within the student community.

Why should you join Horizon?

In Horizon Club, members not only acquire technical skills but also excel in public speaking, professional communication, leadership, and more. Mirroring a small company with departments and ranks, this transformative journey equips participants with a comprehensive skill set, ensuring success in various aspects of their lives.
