Our Main Events

Ideator

Ideator is the biggest startup competition in West Algeria, breaking records with two successful editions. Participants with innovative IT or ICT projects pitch their ideas for a chance to be selected for an exclusive incubation program, provided by our partner, ANPT (Agence Nationale de Promotion et de Développement des Parcs Technologiques). Sponsored by Algérie Télécom, Ideator offers valuable prizes for the winners in the grand finale. Don’t miss the opportunity to turn your idea into the next big startup!

Alumni day

Alumni Day has become a proud tradition at ENSTTIC, and it’s now the biggest alumni event in Algeria. In its 9th edition, this event brings together past ENSTTIC students to share their experiences and insights. As part of the celebration, we host an exciting ICT competition powered by our partner, Lablabee. Sponsored by Algérie Télécom Satellite, Alumni Day continues to foster connection, growth, and innovation among our vibrant community.
