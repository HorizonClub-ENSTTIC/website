NetGenius

NetGenius is a specialized training program launched in 2024, now in its second season. It offers a wide range of training sessions covering: IP Networks Computer Science & Programming Operating Systems Telecommunications AI & Machine Learning Web Development Graphic Design & Photography Video Editing...
Sessions are available both online and in-class, providing flexibility for learners.

In addition to regular sessions, NetGenius hosts three intensive training camps:
NetGenius Winter Camp
NetGenius Ramadan Camp
NetGenius Summer Camp
These camps offer more focused training, conducted online through our Discord server.
